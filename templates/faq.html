Frequently Asked Questions

To purchase tickets, simply browse our events, select the event you're interested in, choose your ticket type, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.

Refund policies vary by event. Please check the specific event's details for refund information. Generally, refunds are available up to 48 hours before the event start time, unless otherwise stated.

After purchasing, your tickets will be available in your account under "My Tickets". You can access them on your mobile device or print them out. Most venues accept mobile tickets, but please check the event details for specific requirements.
